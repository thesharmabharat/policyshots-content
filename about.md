# About Policy Shots

Policy Shots is a small, independent news reader focused on one thing:
making Indian policy, economy, and business news easy to actually
understand — without the jargon.

## What we cover

- **Policy** — laws, regulations, and the government decisions that affect
  everyday life.
- **Business** — what companies and industries are actually doing, and why.
- **Economy** — the numbers behind the headlines, explained simply.
- **Start up** — India's founders, funding, and the businesses they're
  building.
- **Capital Markets** — longer reads on investing, IPOs, and how markets
  actually work.

## Why we exist

Most news is written for people who already understand the context. We
write for people who don't have time to become experts, but still want to
know what's going on and why it matters.

## Get in touch

Have feedback, a correction, or a story idea? Use the **Feedback** option in
the app menu — we read every message.
