## Why this is back in the news

Talk of simplifying the GST structure is back. The goal: fewer slabs, less
confusion, and a system that's easier for small businesses to comply with.

## The proposed slabs

Today's multiple rates can make pricing feel arbitrary. The proposal aims to
**collapse the middle slabs** so most everyday goods fall into one predictable
band.

- Essentials stay low or zero-rated.
- A single "standard" rate for most goods and services.
- A higher rate reserved for luxury and "sin" goods.

## The trade-offs

Simpler isn't automatically cheaper. Some goods get cheaper, a few get costlier,
and the government has to protect its revenue. That balancing act is exactly why
this reform keeps getting debated rather than done.
